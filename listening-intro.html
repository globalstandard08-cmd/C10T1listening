LISTENING TEST - INTRODUCTION

About the Listening Test

Test Format

  • The Listening test has 4 parts with 10 questions each — 40 questions total.
  • You will hear each recording only once.
  • Recordings include conversations, monologues, and talks in a range of accents.
  • You have time to read the questions before each recording starts.
  • While listening, write your answers directly on the screen.

Instructions

  • Answer all questions. There is no penalty for incorrect answers.
  • Write answers clearly. Spelling and grammar must be correct.
  • You can change your answers at any time while you are on the page.
  • Answers are saved automatically as you type.
Teaching Mode: This version does not include a countdown timer. You may take as much time as needed to explain instructions, play audio, review answers, and discuss each part.
